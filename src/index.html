Introduction

  • Brief Story

    The player and protagonist is a Sensei who was summoned from outside the academic city-state of Kivotos by the president of the General Student Council, an extrajudicial committee governing the culturally rich regional school districts. Following her sudden disappearance, criminal activity rises around Kivotos and the player is tasked by the remaining members of the council to resolve issues that crop up and help search for the president.

  • Schools

    Abydos/Arius/Gehenna/Hyakkiyako/Millennium/Red Winter/Shanhaijing/SRT/Trinity/Valkyrie/Highlander/Wildhunt
